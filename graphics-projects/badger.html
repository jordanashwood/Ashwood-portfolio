Badger Labs

Scope:

Badger Labs was an established company that wanted to modernize their brand and create collateral material that reflected their new updated look.

They originally just wanted collateral and a new website design. After creating these items, they loved the look and feel and decided they wanted to update their logo too!

My Process

The Concept

Badger Labs was established in 1966 as Badger Laboratories & Engineering Inc. They had found a new office for their headquarters in Neenah, WI and wanted to update their website and collateral materials to a more modern look.

I was tasked with creating the design for the new website, tradeshow banner, folder with inserts, and a brochure. Once I created these items, Badger Labs decided they also wanted to update their logo.

The Logo

Badger Labs previous logo was a bit wide and dated making it difficult to translate across different medias of various sizes. I wanted to choose a more modern and organic font to give a bold, trustworthy, and clean feel to the brand.

I chose to stick with a blue and green color scheme. Blue represents meanings of trust, loyalty, sincerity, confidence, stability, and intelligence. Green represents growth, positivity and safety while also signifying renewal and abundance. These characteristics are very important to an environmental testing & water sampling company.

I developed multiple logo concepts for them to choose from, and they ended up choosing a combination of a couple logos that I melded together to create the final version.

To keep aligned with the new branding, I wanted to design a website that felt light, clean and natural. I met with the client and noted their main purpose of the site and all of the necessary pages for the sitemap.

Project:

Midwest Carriers

Scope:

As a freelance designer, I was tasked to create multiple different items for Midwest Carriers. The materials include social media posts, signage, and printed materials.

Skills:
  • Branding
  • Layout Design
  • Typography

My Process

The Concept

Over the years, I have been privileged to work with many outstanding companies as a freelancer and I consider Midwest Carriers to be one of the best. They are committed to giving their drivers time to be home with their families and to become part of a passionate family that has been in the industry for more than 35 years.


With an already well-established brand, I was tasked with translating their vision across multiple different types of media. This includes social media posts/cover images, promotional materials, raceway ads, printed materials, and more!

Social Media Posts

Midwest Carriers uses a variety of different themes for their social media posts. They love to use their own images to bring more of a personal touch to their audience, but also have a certain style of vector graphics used in order to provide visual contrast in their posts.

Midwest Man

Midwest Carriers wanted to create a mascot to use throughout social media to bring playfulness to some of their posts. I created Midwest using the Midwest orange and a complimentary blue and many "comic book" style templates to be used as needed.

Promotional Materials

Midwest Carriers holds many annual events for their employees which typically involves passing out swag as well. I was able to create hats, promo flags, invites, and more!

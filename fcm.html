Project:

Fox Cities Magazine

Scope:

As the Art Director of Fox Cities Magazine, I was responsible for designing each montly issue from start to finish. I coordinated with the sales executives on deadlines for advertisements and designed each ad included in the issue. I would also travel around Wisconsin taking photos to accompany articles and worked with printers to ensure issues were printed accurately and on time.

Skills:
  • Ad Design
  • Branding
  • Layout Design
  • Photography
  • Typography

My Process

The Concept

FOX CITIES Magazine is committed to sharing the experience of life in Northeast Wisconsin, including food & dining, people, history, homes, health & wellness, the arts and, the all important


The Fox Cities are 19 communities in the greater Appleton area along the Fox River in East Central Wisconsin. One of the state's fastest growing areas with about 250,000 residents, the Fox Cities offer a thriving metropolitan area closely surrounded by incredible natural beauty.

The City Guide

Annually, Fox Cities Magazine releases a new City Guide. This piece is very helpful for new residents of the Fox Cities. Businesses all over the Fox Cities purchase ads that are to be seemlessly integrated into the design and layout of the guide. It includes information on:

  • Community Profiles
  • Local Food & Brews
  • Arts & Culture
  • Involvement
  • Diversity
  • Getting Around
  • Child Care
  • Economy, Housing & Taxes

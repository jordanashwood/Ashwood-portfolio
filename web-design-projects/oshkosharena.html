Project:

Oshkosh Arena

Scope:

The arena boasts a multitude of spaces suitable for a range of celebratory events, concerts, private gatherings, and sporting tournaments! From the Arena Bowl welcoming up to 4,200 fans, the Oshkosh Arena accommodates it all.

When the arena finished construction in 2017, they came to us in need of a modern and responsive site to show all of their upcoming events!

Skills:
  • Branding
  • Layout Design
  • Typography
  • Web Design

My Process

The Homepage

Oshkosh Arena, formerly known as the Menominee Nation Arena, wanted a modern new site that was easy to navigate.

For the design, I wanted to create a dynamic feel using diagonal elements that mimicked spotlights used for large events. We wanted to keep the homepage simple and have a rotating hero image, highlight upcoming events, and provide a space for articles/announcements.

Interior

I also created a mockup of an internal page to show the developers an example of the text heirarchy and form fields. On the right side, we included a section to display a couple of the upcoming events in order to stay visible to the user.

Project:

Top Dog Apparel

Scope:

Top Dog, now STRYKER, has been supplying the area’s toughest workers and business leaders with high-quality custom branded apparel. STRYKER offers the toughest brands in the industry to best suit their clients’ most demanding conditions.

Since the founding of STRYKER in 1960, the focus of their products has been on durability and customization. They offer a lot of services and wanted a site that would showcase that.

Skills:
  • Branding
  • Layout Design
  • Typography
  • Web Design

My Process

The Homepage

In the kickoff meeting, I had asked the customer what they wanted highlighted on the homepage. They asked to include the services they offer, a showcase section, their contact info and catalogs.

For their hero area, I created a grid of images that when hovered would show which service was used and a link to that service page. They already had bright colors incorporated in their logo, so I wanted to stick with bright "poppy" colors to keep the user excited about the products. I also included icons to assist with visual cues and keep the site looking modern.

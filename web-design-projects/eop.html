Project:

Edge of Park

Scope:

Since 1968 Edge of Park Bicycle and Moped Rental has operated as a family run business providing the ideal way to experience Peninsula State Park. When ownership changed in 2015, the new owners wanted to step up their digital presence by redesigning their website.

Skills:
  • Layout Design
  • Typography
  • Web Design

My Process

The Homepage

Prior to the kickoff meeting with the client, I had sent them a questionnaire asking about their vision and brand. I had asked specifics on imagery, style, font, colors and more. I wanted to keep the red from the logo and add other bright colors to keep the site bright and positive.

AHA already had wonderful photos that I could pull from and I wanted to add iconography to bring the site into a more modern space. All of these aspects also led me to choose a simple, yet bold typeface to tie the site together.
